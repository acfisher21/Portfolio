About

I am a web designer, teacher, and trail runner. My journey into tech started as a desire to learn a new skill and has developed into a freelancing career. I believe that in this ever evolving world, techonology is the common language I can use to communicate and create for myself and others. I love learning and building things; I have been for as long as I remember.

As a result, I chose a career in website development to merge my passions for learning, creativity, and building. Outside of work I teach nutrition and gardening education to elementary students. I love trail running, rock climbing, and mountain biking! I spend my free time playing in the Ozark Mountains of central Arkansas.
